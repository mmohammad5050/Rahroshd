Translate is a free language translation system that was founded by Google. This system is accessible through the web, application, browser extension and augmented reality. Translate can translate words, phrases, and web pages without cost between Persian and more than 100 other languages instantly. Translate can also translate orally, visually, audibly and even handwriting. Translate uses artificial intelligence and deep learning technology to improve the quality of its translations. Translate is one of the most popular and widely used services of Google that can help you get familiar with the world around you and communicate in different languages.
