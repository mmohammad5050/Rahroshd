Application design means the process of transforming an idea or a need into a software that can run on smart devices such as mobile phones, tablets and computers. Application design consists of several steps, which are as follows: - Step one: Identify the need and the idea. In this step, you have to define a problem or a need and examine whether your application can solve or fulfill it. You have to identify your target market, competitors, advantages and disadvantages of your application and provide a business plan. - Step two: Design the application. In this step, you have to design the appearance and functionality of your application. You have to choose the user interface, icons, colors, fonts, menus, buttons and other graphical elements and determine how the user interacts with the application. You also have to consider the software architecture, database, algorithms, security and other technical aspects. - Step three: Develop the application. In this step, you have to code your application. You have to select the programming language, tools and frameworks that are suitable for your platform. You also have to pay attention that your application complies with the standards, specifications and requirements of the platform. - Step four: Test the application. In this step, you have to test your application on different devices and in different conditions. You have to ensure the quality, performance, security, compatibility and user satisfaction of your application. You also have to collect and analyze the feedback of the users and fix the errors and shortcomings. - Step five: Publish the application. In this step, you have to register and publish your application in the relevant stores such as Google Play and App Store. You have to follow the rules and regulations of the stores and provide the necessary documents and information. You also have to take actions for marketing and advertising your application and find ways to attract and retain the users.
